Gett (Get taxi) 

Sadly, we don't have Uber in Israel and taxis can be very expensive and sadly, many tourists get scammed by the taxi drivers in Israel. This app is great for many reasons; you can choose to pay by credit card and not have to deal with trying to find the right coins or bills to pay, you also get a map of the route from where you're getting picked up to where you want to go that way you know the driver isn't taking you in circles just to make more money.

 

Waze

If you aren't familiar with this app yet, you should be. This is the best app while driving in Israel. It shows you the quickest routes and alarms when there are police around or hazards.

Pango

Parking app. This App is great if you're renting a car in Israel because it is responsible for all parking services in Israel, especially for parking on city streets.

Wolt

I don't know about you but have you ever been to a foreign country and felt like ordering food because you were too tired to go out, but didn't know how to order? This app is great if you want food delivery from some of the best places.

 

Moovit

This app offers guidance on how to use public transportation to get you from one point to another if it's by train, bus, and walking, they connect it all.

 

xe currency

In Israel, we use the NIS (New Israeli Shekel) It is easier to use this app in order to know how much you're spending in your currency.

 

KVISI

If you're looking for a place to clean clothes this app is for you. This app provides laundry services and offers free pick up and delivery of the clothes.
