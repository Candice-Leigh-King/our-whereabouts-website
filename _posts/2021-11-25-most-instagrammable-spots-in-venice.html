Dan and I flew to Venice in September 2021 with low expectations and we quickly fell in love with this city. We finally understand why 5 MILLION people visit this beautiful city each year. There are so many beautiful spots in this city here are some that you shouldn't miss

- _section-type: blog-textarea textarea_html: >-

1. San Marco at sunrise

We woke up early to catch the sunrise at St. Mark's Square. It is one of the most popular spots in the city therefore arrive early to beat the crowds. There is something extremely romantic and beautiful about watching the sunrise here.

2. Rialto bridge

You'll walk another 450m and reach the magnificent Ponte di Rialto. There are many ways to admire this bridge.


Coordinations to this spot:


45.4384198, 12.3359621

 

3. Libreria Acqua Alta

The most unique bookstore you'll ever visit. The magic starts the moment you enter. You'll find hidden gems all around, friendly cats everywhere, a gondola in the middle of the store and at the end of it is a photo op. Opening hours: Daily, 9 AM – 7:45 PM.

 

4. Bridge of Sighs

There are four ways you can see this magnificent bridge.

There are 4 different angles where you can capture a photo with this bridge:

  • Option 1: Ponte della Paglia: It's a bridge right next to St. Mark's square and just behind the Doge's Palace.
  • Option 2: Below Ponte della Pagila (in photo): When facing the Bridge of Sighs walk to your right off the bridge and make a left turn to an alley for this cool spot in this photo.
  • Option 3: The bridge near Trattoria Canonica‏ restaurant.
  • Option 4: Gondola ride. This is a great option, but an expensive one. (Most expensive option..)

 

5. Basilica di San Marco

We booked and reserved a spot online to enter the basilica ahead of time. You can make a reservation online starting in April until November. The rest of the months the que isn't too bad so you can't preorder tickets. We recommend you go inside if possible, it is beautiful.

  • Entrance fee: 3 Euro per adult.
  • Opening hours: Daily 9:35 AM – 5:00 PM.

 

6. Hidden pier near Hotel San Moise

This small pier is an Instagram famous spot that has no name. To get here you need to enter 'Hotel San Moise' into google maps. The pier gives you a wonderful view of Calle Dario la Chiesa bridge and if you don't know about this spot, you'll probably miss it because it isn't noticeable.

7. Scala Contarini del Bovolo

Not far is Palazzo Contarini del Bovolo. It is a small palazzo in Venice, best known for its external multi-arch spiral staircase with a spectacular view of the city. Don't forget to buy tickets ahead of time!

  • Entrance Fee: 7 Euro.
  • Opening hours: Every day, 10:00 AM to 18:00 PM.

8. Burano

Located 50 minutes away from Venice are two islands you shouldn't miss during your visit. It's a charming place, with a calmer pace than Venice where you are able to get away from the crowds. It is a group of islands within the Venice district that are made up of several small islands connected together by a series of canals and bridges, similar to Venice.
