As you scroll through social media and you see all these beautiful shots people take with these unique and stunning bridges one might think that they are all noticeable and easy to find. Well that is not the case. Did you know that there are 400 bridges in Venice? Yes, that is a large amount of bridges. I'm here to help you out with find these beautiful bridges.

I added a map that I created here of the distracts in Venice so it could help you.

CALLE DRIO CHIESA BRIDGE

District: San Marco.
Location: Near Teatro La Fenice.

Enter 'Hotel San Moise' into your google maps. Once you see the hotel on your right you'll find a small dock on your left where this photo was taken. Not many people know about this spot and the location of it makes it invisible to those who aren't aware of this spot.

Coordinates:

45.433443, 12.335059

PONTE MARIA CALLAS BRIDGE

District: San Marco.
Location: Near Teatro La Fenice

This beautiful bridge on a very small canal is hidden between the buildings, which creates a very cozy and picturesque spot. What adds even more charm to it is the Santo Stefano bell tower behind.
