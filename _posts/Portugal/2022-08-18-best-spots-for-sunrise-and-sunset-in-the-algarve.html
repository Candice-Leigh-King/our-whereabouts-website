The Algarve coast, located in southern Portugal, is a paradise for beach lovers and surfers. Here is a list of the most beautiful spots in the Algarve to watch the sunrise and sunset.

Lagoa:

  • Praia da Marinha - best time to visit sunrise and sunset

  • Praia da Mesquita - best time is sunset

  • Capela da Nossa Senhora da Rocha - sunrises and sunsets are magical here. It's a small chapel on a cliff.

- _section-type: blog-featured-textarea blog-featured-textarea_html: >-

Lagos:

  • Lagos Roman Bridge - Sunrise

  • Praia Dona Ana - Sunrise

  • Praia do Camilo - Sunrise
  • Ponta da Piedade - best time to visit is sunrise
  • Porto de Mos - best spot for sunset
- _section-type: blog-textarea textarea_html: >-

Carvoeiro:

  • Algar Seco - Sunset
  • Praia de Carvoeiro - sunrise
  • Albandeira Beach - Sunrise here is magical.

Alvor: 

  • Praia dos Três Irmãos - Best time to visit is sunrise because of the colors and the tide is low.

Albufeira: 

  • Praia da Falésia - Sunset is best but you can also enjoy the sunrise here.

Sagres: 

  • Cabo São Vicente - sunsets here are magical.
