Lisbon is the perfect city for romantic getaways, solo travelers, beach vacations, and foodies. It is a charming seaside destination with so much history, but also a combination of cosmopolitan.

If you're looking for the most Instagrammable spots in Lisbon, here's a great list including when you should visit them.

- _section-type: blog-textarea textarea_html: >-

Miradouro de Santa Luzia

 

This romantic terrace is located by the church of Santa Luzia and provides a beautiful view of Alfama district with a view over its houses, churches, and the Tagus River.
The best time to visit this beautiful viewpoint is during sunset. Sunrises here aren't the greatest time to visit for photography but are definitely beautiful and less crowded.

- _section-type: blog-cover-image blog-cover_image: /uploads/img-6737.jpg - _section-type: blog-featured-textarea blog-featured-textarea_html: >-

Carvoeiro Boardwalk and Algar Seco

  • Region: Lagoa, South Coast
  • Distance: 1km
  • Average duration: 1h

You can extend the Seven Hanging Valleys hike by walking further to the Algar Seco caves and Carvoeiro Boardwalk. If you're too tired you can always drive to Algar Seco and Carvoeiro Boardwalk by car.  They are both amazing places to explore.

- _section-type: blog-window right_image: /uploads/23.jpg left_image: /uploads/20220504-075814-2.jpg - _section-type: blog-textarea textarea_html: >-

Castelo de São Jorge

 

The Castelo de São Jorge is one of Lisbon's most distinctive monuments, is situated on the city's highest hill which gives you an endless view over the city. 
The best time to visit is early morning between 8 am - 12 am.

- _section-type: blog-cover-image blog-cover_image: /uploads/32.jpg - _section-type: blog-featured-textarea blog-featured-textarea_html: >-

Se de Lisboa


The Cathedral of Saint Mary Major (Portuguese: Santa Maria Maior de Lisboa or Metropolitan Cathedral of St. Mary Major), is often called Lisbon Cathedral or simply the Sé (Sé de Lisboa).
The best time to visit the Cathedral is early morning or late afternoon since it is the most popular cathedral in Lisbon.
